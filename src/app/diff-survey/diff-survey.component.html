About the creator of BLogDiff

Hi! My name is Noah and I am currently finishing my bachelor's degree in business informatics/information systems. One of the research questions I want to answer in my thesis is: Does a specialised differencing tool support a developer reading a build log? To evaluate this I developed this tool and urge you to try it out! I am relying on people working in development or operations to quickly test the tool and give me a short feedback. By taking part in my survey, you would really help me out! If that's not enough to motivate you, I'll also give away one 50$ gift card of choice for those taking part ;)

Please feel free to share the survey with your friends!

About the survey

The survey consists out of 29 questions (4 open, 25 closed) and roughly takes about 3 minutes.

The data collected will be used for scientific purposes exclusively! Participation in the survey is voluntary, but by participating you give us permission that we may use and publish the results in a scientific study.
